Iridescence Interest Group

A branding project I took on as a Founder and Creative Media Director while managing the social media presence and designing all graphic materials for all events.

What is Iridescence?
Iridescence is an interest group for a non-exclusive, Asian-interest sorority focused on spreading cultural awareness, service, and professional development.

➤The task: Distinguish Iridescence from other school organizations while promoting the group’s aims and objectives.

➤The solution: Gather input from each member to create a brand that reflects the group as a whole.

➤The result: High engagement rate with followers, increased interactions with student body and improved communications with students, staff and other organizations, even those outside the university.

 

Style Guide

The Story Behind the Logo:

Each of our members come from different backgrounds with different stories and different personalities. When we come together, we shine just like how different colors come together to make something iridescent. (a little cheesy, I know)

I also included gladiolus flowers, but not just for aesthetic purposes. Due to their association with gladiators, they symbolize strength, sincerity and moral integrity. This really resonates with our group as a whole and thus the logo was born.
